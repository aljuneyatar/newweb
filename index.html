About



Welcome to Bugsay Comscie, where we explore the vast and dynamic field of computer science. Our goal is to provide in-depth insights and resources on various topics, from programming languages and algorithms to artificial intelligence and cybersecurity.

The Bachelor of Science in Computer Science equips students with essential knowledge, skills, and values to create top-notch software, innovative algorithms, and commercially viable computing innovations, while also fostering impactful Corporate Social Responsibility programs and producing competitive graduates meeting industry and community needs.
